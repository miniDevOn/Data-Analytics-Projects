Humidity

At the time of data collection, February 10th, 2018, there appears to be very little correlation between Latitude and Humidity across cities. What we do notice, is that between Latitudes of 0 degreees and -20 degrees, there appears to be a small grouping of cities all very near 100% Humidity. For Latitudes between 40 degrees and 80 degrees, there are many cities with Humidities between 100% and 60%, which may be effected by time of day as well as season.
